About Career Craft

Learn more about what we built, why we built it, and who it's for.

What is Career Craft?

Career Craft is a free online ATS resume builder designed to help job seekers create professional, clean, and recruiter-ready CVs without any signup or payment.

We built this tool for students, fresh graduates, and working professionals who need a fast, reliable way to format their resume so it passes both automated screening systems and human review.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple — give every job seeker access to a professional resume builder that works on any device, requires no account, and produces a clean PDF instantly.

We continuously improve the platform by adding new templates, ATS optimizations, and formatting improvements based on real hiring trends.

Why We Built This

Most resume builders charge money, require signups, or produce poorly-formatted output that fails ATS filters. Career Craft exists to remove those barriers. Whether you are applying for your first job or switching careers, your resume should not be the reason you get rejected.
